The whole thing in one breath

What the heck is this?

For 25 years, BioSolveIT has built software that helps scientists design new medicines on a computer, long before anything touches a lab bench. Brilliant, invisible, important work.

The problem for the next 25 years is not the science. It is getting anyone to notice it. A teenager who will be a scientist one day learns from a 30-second video, not a 40-page PDF. So this tool asks a simple question about any post, email, or caption:

"Will this actually land with the person reading it?"

It reads your words, gives them a score out of 100, and tells you the one thing to fix. That is it. That is the whole tool.
