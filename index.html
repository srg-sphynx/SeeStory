Paste a draft, pick your audience, get a score and the one fix that helps most.

1. Pick who it is for

Pick the one group you are writing for. The tool changes what it rewards based on this choice. You can switch it any time to see how the same draft scores for a different reader.

2. Paste your draft

Paste the real words you plan to send or post, not a description of them. The tool reads as you type. It reads words only, so visuals go in Step 3.

3. Tick what your draft includes

Tick what is true. The tool already finds numbers and calls to action on its own. Be honest, since a box you did not include gives a score you cannot trust.

Then read the result

The score and the one highest-impact fix update on every change. Apply the fix, look again, and stop at green. The score is guidance, never a gate.
