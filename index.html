The Resonance Engine

The chemistry metaphor

Think of your audience as a binding pocket and your content as a small molecule. Each audience has a unique shape defined by four Resonance Classes: Clarity, Voice, Visual, and Community. Your message fragments are molecular fragments that fit into the pocket.

How scoring works

The Resonance Score blends two signals. Pocket fit measures how well your selected fragments match the audience weights (0 to 45 points). Text analysis scans your caption for drivers that boost the score and show-stoppers that hurt it (plus or minus 15 points). A base score of 40 anchors the scale.

Drivers and show-stoppers

Drivers are good signals: short sentences, concrete numbers, clear calls to action. Show-stoppers are red flags: hyperbole, em dashes, run-on sentences, and all-caps shouting. The Story-Activity Spotter flags both in real time.

Sharing results

Every change updates the URL hash. Copy the link to share your exact configuration. Drafts are also saved to local storage automatically.
