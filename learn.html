The shift

Your audience changed. The whitepaper did not.

📱

They scroll, they don't subscribe

The incoming generation of scientists rarely reads journals, listens to science radio, or watches feature-length documentaries. Short video on TikTok and Instagram is where they meet new ideas first.

🎬

Research summaries win

Across science accounts, the videos that summarise a real paper draw the highest engagement - demand from audiences who were never going to open the PDF.

🤝

Trust follows a face

Following a credible science creator measurably raised trust in science for most viewers. People follow people - not logos.

⚖️

But accessible can over-sell

Simplified science can also breed overconfidence. The win is clarity with substance - which is exactly the balance the four signals are tuned for.
